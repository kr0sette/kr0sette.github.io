About Me

Kristine Rosette Enerio is a 27-year-old woman living in Union, N.J. By day she manages publisher relations for an online academic database called JSTOR and has been with the organization for five years.

By night, she is an avid fan of indie table top role playing games and GM's for a weekly gaming group that meets at her house. The group is wrapping up a month-long campaign for the game Velvet Glove, where players each play a member of a teenage girl gang in the 1970s. On occasion she also attends a LARP or two, though it is not her favorite medium of story telling.

At the moment, she is enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp where she is quickly gaining knowledge in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. She hopes to eventually land a project management position, and wants to use these skills to better inform her of how developers work. For now, she simply enjoys creating something again.
