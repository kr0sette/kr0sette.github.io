Fruit Picking

Instructions

Hit the play button and you will be given a random budget at the start of the game.

There are four types of fruit below. By clicking on a fruit you will purchase that fruit at a specific price that contributes to your total.

You will win the game by matching your total to your budget. You lose the game if your total goes above the budget amount.

The price of each fruit is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the price of each fruit.
