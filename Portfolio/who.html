About Me


Hello! I am a budding web developer looking to apply her expertise in digital academic publishing and journalism to a tech setting.

Currently, I work in New York as a Senior Publisher Support Specialist at JSTOR, where I manage relationships between the online library and its partnering publishers. Since 2013, my work has contributed to ITHAKA's greater mission of increasing accessibility to knowledge by developing and improving digital learning tools.

I graduated from Rutgers University in 2012 with a Bachelor's degree in Political Science and Journalism. During that time, I spent two years as an editor for the Daily Targum practicing my passion for story telling and developing my interpersonal skills.

Now I am growing my skills in HTML/CSS and javascript in order to become more involved with the technical aspects of my career background. If you're interested in working together, please don't hesitate to contact me.
